What is $BANA & What Can I Do With It?

 

$BANA is ApeX Pro's native reward token, distributed exclusively via ApeX Pro's year-long Trade-to-Earn event. Traders accumulate their USDC perpetual contract trades every week and receive $BANA tokens for the trades they made during that week. Now with this limited-time airdrop, ApeX Pro is giving users a chance to earn $BANA outside of the Trade-to-Earn event for the very first time.

 

 

Make your $BANA work for you in two ways.

  1. Hold $BANA & Redeem $APEX: At the end of the Trade-to-Earn campaign (end 2023), $BANA holders can convert their $BANA to ApeX Pro's native governance token $APEX pro-rata. The redemption rate is not fixed; as Trade-to-Earn progresses, the amount of $APEX that users can redeem with the same amount of $BANA will increase. $APEX can also be traded on Bybit and Uniswap.
  2. Join Trade-to-Earn on ApeX Pro: After creating an ApeX Pro account, users can join Trade-to-Earn and accumulate trading volume for even more $BANA! Users can also exchange their earned $BANA from Trade-to-Earn for $APEX. Additionally, Trade-to-Earn participants can either swap $BANA for USDC or add liquidity to the $BANA-USDC Pool to earn LP Tokens, that will help them multiply their $BANA holdings.
